
Backends:

- VULKAN 

- METAL

- DX12

- DX11

- GL

- BROWSER_WEBGPU (supported when targeting the web through WebAssembly)

- PRIMIRAY (all the APIs that wgpu offers first tier of support for: Vulkan + Metal + DX12 + Browser WebGPU)

- SECONDARY (All the APIs that wgpu offers second tier of support for: OpenGL + DX11)

- all() (returns all backends)
